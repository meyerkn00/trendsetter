We are asking you to participate in a research study. This study is being led by Cristina Bicchieri, a professor at the University of Pennsylvania.

What the Study is About

The purpose of this research is to learn about social decision-making. We will ask you to complete a brief survey either before or after you are asked to iteratively choose colors from a list of three (green, purple, and yellow) alongside other members of your assigned group.

Risks and Discomforts

We do not anticipate any risks from participating in this research.

Benefits

Information from this study may benefit other people now or in the future by providing useful insights about decision-making in groups.

Compensation for Participation

Subjects will be compensated between $4 and $6.80 for their participation, depending on performance.

Privacy/Confidentiality/Data Security

The data collected will not be identifiable and cannot be linked to you personally. Any data we collect will be stored in a password protected folder to which only the researchers have access. We anticipate that your participation in this experiment presents no greater risk than everyday use of a computer.

Sharing De-identified Data Collected in this Research

De-identified data from this study may be shared with the research community at large to advance science. No one will be able to identify you from the information we share. Your participation in this research is voluntary. You have the right to withdraw at any point during the study, for any reason, and without any prejudice. If you have questions The contact for this study is Jack Goldberg, a research specialist at the University of Pennsylvania. If you have questions, you may contact Jack at goldbe3@upenn.edu. If you have any questions or concerns regarding your rights as a subject in this study at any time before, during or after participation, please contact the Institutional Review Board (IRB) at (215) 898-2614 for assistance.

Statement of Consent

I have read the above information and I consent to take part in the study.
